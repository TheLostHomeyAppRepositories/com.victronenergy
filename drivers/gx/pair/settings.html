Setup Instructions

Before connecting, please ensure:

  1. Enable Modbus TCP: Modbus TCP is disabled by default on GX devices. You must manually enable it by navigating to Settings → Services → Modbus TCP and setting it to Enabled.
  2. Find your Modbus Unit ID: On your GX device, go to Settings → Services → Modbus/TCP → Available services to find the correct unit ID for your GX device (typically starts with com.victronenergy.vebus).
  3. Network Access: Ensure your Homey can reach the GX device on the specified IP address and port (default: 502).
